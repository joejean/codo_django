Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

Q: How long does the review process take?

At CrowdEmpowered we do our best to review your campaign as soon as possible. But please allow a maximum of three days to hear from US. You will be notified when the campaign is approved.

Q: Can I submit more than one campaign the same day?

Yes this is completely fine as long they are two different campaigns.

Q: What happens after my campaign gets accepted?

After your campaign gets accepted it will go live on the website and donors will be able to start contributing right away.

Q: How long should my campaign be run?

30 days is generally a good number of days to have a program running. However, you are free to pick a slightly lower or slightly higher time frame.
